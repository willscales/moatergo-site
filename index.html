The Problem

Sitting is the new smoking.
But "sit up straight" isn't the answer.

Most posture tools buzz when you slouch. That's like an alarm that only tells you the building is already on fire. Real ergonomic risk is cumulative — it builds over hours, days, and weeks.

How It Works

Continuous measurement.
Actionable intelligence.

MoatErgo uses wearable sensors to capture how your body actually moves and holds position throughout the day — then translates that data into clear, actionable guidance.

1

Wear

Lightweight sensors mount to your upper body using a comfortable, low-profile harness designed for all-day wear over clothing. Calibrate once — it remembers your baseline.

2

Measure

Real-time motion data is continuously captured and analyzed. Not just "are you slouching" — but how much load, for how long, and where the risk is building.

3

Improve

Get specific guidance on what to change — your chair height, monitor angle, break timing — based on your actual measured exposure, not generic advice.
