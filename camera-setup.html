Camera Workspace Setup

Use your phone's rear camera to measure your workspace. You'll calibrate with a sheet of paper, then measure your monitor distance, height, and screen tilt.

Requires camera access over HTTPS. Works in Safari on iOS 15+. The camera is only used for measurement -- no photos are stored.
