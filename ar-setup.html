AR Workspace Measurement

Use your camera to place markers in augmented reality and measure your desk, chair, and monitor precisely.

WebXR requires HTTPS. This will not work over plain HTTP (except localhost on some browsers). Chrome on Android has the best support. Safari on iOS requires iOS 15+ with WebXR enabled in Settings > Safari > Advanced > Feature Flags.
